About Us

On one of our first dates, my wife-to-be hopped on my motorcycle and took off while eating an ice cream, riding around my parents’ yard with one hand as she ate the ice cream as if she’d been riding all her life. Unfortunately, we hadn’t gone over the fine art of stopping, and suddenly—BOOM. She was off. But up she bounced full of ice cream all over her shirt and pants, announcing, “That was fun!” Then and there, I knew this was the girl for me and that ice cream disaster would have to turn into a life long memory and business.

Artisanal

Frosting Dream imports flavorings from small farmers around the world — pistachios from Syria, coffee from Guatemala, chocolate from Colombia — and now grows many ingredients on its organic farm outside Turin, where its sole factory is also located.
